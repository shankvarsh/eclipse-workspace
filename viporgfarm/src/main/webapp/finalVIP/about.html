VIP – Scope & Offered Services

VIP, built with an objective to fast-track mobile governance in the country, is a common unified platform, common unified mobile application/website, providing citizens/residents a single point access to major government services through mobile device. On-boarding of different citizen-centric government services on VIP is envisaged to be a continuous process that shall result in increasing number of such services on VIP with the passage of time. Through the VIP mobile application, You can access pan-India citizen/resident-centric government services of Your choice, from any location, at any time, offered by central/state/union territory government departments, local bodies and their agencies and utility services from corporates (herein after referred to as “Service Providers”). Access to VIP is public. You can register and create the VIP account using Your mobile number.
